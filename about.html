ABOUT GINGERSNAPS

What is this place? Why are you here?

THE CONCEPT

Gingersnaps is a web project that blends cutecore aesthetics with horror elements. It's an interactive experience that challenges the boundary between innocent childhood nostalgia and digital dread.

Like the retro websites of old (nekoweb, neosite), Gingersnaps is a space where you can engage with games, care for a virtual companion, and discover hidden secrets.

CUTECORE VS HORROR

The website intentionally juxtaposes two aesthetics:

  • CUTECORE: Pastel colors, adorable pixel art, innocent interactions
  • HORROR: Glitch effects, eerie atmosphere, unsettling undertones

The tension between these creates an unsettling experience that's both comforting and disturbing.

THE GAMES

TWIST & TURNS: A narrative game that follows a seemingly innocent story with dark twists and shocking revelations.

DRESS-UP: Customize your girl with cute and horrifying outfits. Mix and match to express yourself.

VIRTUAL PET: Care for your digital companion. Feed her, play with her, love her. But neglect has consequences...

TECHNICAL

Built with: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Aesthetic inspiration: Neosite/Nekoweb, Y2K internet culture, Vaporwave, Glitch Art

This is a passion project exploring nostalgia, web design, and interactive horror.
